{% trans "What is the Auto Lease Calculator?" %}

{% blocktrans %}This calculator estimates your monthly car lease payment using the standard lease formula: depreciation plus finance charge (rent charge), plus sales tax. You enter MSRP, negotiated price, lease term, residual percentage (of MSRP), and money factor. The money factor is the lease equivalent of interest rate; multiply by 2400 to get approximate APR.{% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Auto Lease Payment Formula" %}

{% trans "Monthly payment has three parts: depreciation, finance charge, and tax:" %}

Depreciation = (Adjusted Cap Cost − Residual) / Term

Finance charge = (Adjusted Cap Cost + Residual) × Money Factor

Monthly = Depreciation + Finance charge + (base × tax rate)

{% trans "APR equivalent:" %}

{% trans "APR ≈ Money Factor × 2400. Example: 0.00125 × 2400 = 3% APR." %}

{% trans "Key Concepts" %}

{% trans "Money factor" %}

{% trans "The lease equivalent of interest rate. APR ≈ Money Factor × 2400. Lower is better." %}

{% trans "Residual value" %}

{% trans "Estimated value of the car at lease end, usually as a percentage of MSRP. Higher residual means lower monthly payment." %}

{% trans "Capitalized cost" %}

{% trans "The amount being financed: negotiated price plus fees, minus down payment and rebates. Lower cap cost means lower payment." %}
