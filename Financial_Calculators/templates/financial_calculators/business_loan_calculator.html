{% trans "What is the Business Loan Calculator?" %}

{% trans "This calculator estimates payments and costs for business term loans, SBA 7(a) loans, and lines of credit. You can also compare multiple financing options side by side for a given loan amount." %}

{% trans "Term loans and SBA loans show monthly (or periodic) payment, total interest, fees, and total cost. Line of credit mode shows payoff time and total interest for your current balance and payment. Compare mode shows payment and total cost for several common loan scenarios." %}

{% trans "Key Metrics" %}

{% trans "Periodic / Monthly Payment" %}

{% trans "The fixed amount you pay each period. Term loans can be calculated for monthly, biweekly, or weekly frequency." %}

{% trans "Total Interest & Total Cost" %}

{% trans "Total interest is the cost of borrowing. Total cost includes interest plus any origination or guarantee fees." %}

{% trans "SBA Guarantee Fee" %}

{% trans "SBA 7(a) loans may include a guarantee fee based on loan size; the calculator applies a simplified fee structure." %}

  1. {% trans "Choose loan type: Term Loan, SBA Loan, Line of Credit, or Compare Options." %}
  2. {% trans "Enter loan amount, rate, and term (or for LOC: credit limit, balance, rate, payment)." %}
  3. {% trans "For term loans, optionally set origination fee and payment frequency." %}
  4. {% trans "Click Calculate to see payment, total interest, fees, and total cost (or comparison table)." %}

{% trans "Term loan and SBA results show your periodic payment, effective APR, and a summary of loan amount, interest, fees, and total cost.
