{% trans "What is the College Cost Calculator?" %}

{% blocktrans %}This calculator projects how much college will cost in the future based on today's average costs (tuition, room & board, books, other) and an inflation rate. You enter your child's current age, type of college (public in-state, out-of-state, private, or community), and number of years. It shows total projected cost and, if you enter current savings and expected return, how much to save each month to close the gap.{% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Key Concepts" %}

{% trans "Projected cost" %}

{% trans "Today's average cost is inflated each year until the first year of college. Total cost is the sum of all years of college at those projected amounts." %}

{% trans "Funding gap" %}

{% trans "The amount still needed after your current savings grow at the expected return until college starts. Monthly savings needed is calculated to fill this gap." %}

{% trans "Savings projection" %}

{% trans "Shows how your balance grows year by year with assumed contributions and investment return. Use it to see if you're on track." %}

{% trans "How to Use This Calculator" %}

  1. {% trans "Enter your child's current age (0–18). Years until college = 18 minus age." %}
  2. {% trans "Select college type and number of years (e.g. 4). Set an education inflation rate (often 5%% or higher)." %}
  3. {% trans "Optionally enter current savings and expected annual return to see monthly savings needed and projection." %}
  4. {% trans "Click Calculate to see total cost, breakdown, year-by-year costs, and savings plan." %}
  5. {% trans "Use the charts to compare cost by category and by year." %}

{% trans "Understanding Your Results" %}

{% trans "Projected total cost" %}

{% trans "Sum of all years of college at inflated prices. This is the target amount to fund." %}

{% trans "Monthly savings needed" %}

{% trans "The fixed monthly contribution that, with your current savings growing at the expected return, would close the funding gap by the time college starts." %}

{% trans "Cost breakdown and by year" %}

{% trans "First-year cost is split into tuition, room & board, books, other. The year-by-year table shows how each college year's cost increases with inflation." %}

{% trans "Understanding the Charts" %}

{% trans "Cost Breakdown (First Year)" %}

{% trans "Cost by Year" %}
