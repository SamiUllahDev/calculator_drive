{% trans "What is Future Value?" %}

{% blocktrans %}Future value (FV) is the amount your money will grow to over time with compound interest. This calculator combines the growth of a starting lump sum and the future value of regular contributions (e.g. monthly). The more you invest and the longer the period, the larger your future value.{% endblocktrans %}
