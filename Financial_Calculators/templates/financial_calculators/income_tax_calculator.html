{% trans "Key Concepts" %}

{% trans "Marginal vs Effective Rate" %}

{% trans "Marginal rate is the tax on your last dollar of income. Effective rate is total tax divided by gross income—your average tax burden." %}

{% trans "FICA" %}

{% trans "Social Security (6.2% up to the wage base) and Medicare (1.45%, plus 0.9% additional above a threshold). Applied to wages." %}

{% trans "Standard vs Itemized" %}

{% trans "Take the larger of the standard deduction (by filing status) or your total itemized deductions (e.g. mortgage interest, SALT, charity)." %}
