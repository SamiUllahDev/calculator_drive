{% trans "What is Simple vs Compound Interest?" %}

{% blocktrans %}Simple interest is calculated only on the principal. Compound interest is calculated on the principal plus accumulated interest. Compound interest grows faster because you earn "interest on interest."{% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Simple: I = P × r × t" %}

{% trans "Compound: A = P(1 + r/n)^(nt)" %}
