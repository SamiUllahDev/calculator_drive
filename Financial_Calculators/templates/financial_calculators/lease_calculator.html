{% trans "What is the Lease Calculator?" %}

{% blocktrans %}This calculator estimates your monthly equipment lease payment. You enter the equipment cost, lease term in months, interest rate, and residual value. The payment is based on financing the depreciation (cost minus residual) over the term. Fair Market Value (FMV) leases have a residual at the end; $1 buyout leases effectively finance the full cost.{% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Lease Payment Formula" %}

{% trans "The monthly payment finances the amount to be depreciated (cost minus residual):" %}

PMT = (Cost − Residual) / PV factor

{% trans "PV factor = [1 − (1+r)^(−n)] / r — r = monthly rate, n = term in months" %}

{% trans "Key Concepts" %}

{% trans "Residual value" %}

{% trans "Estimated value of the equipment at the end of the lease. Lower residual means higher monthly payments." %}

{% trans "Fair Market Value vs $1 Buyout" %}

{% trans "FMV: you can return the equipment or buy it at market value. $1 buyout: you own the equipment for $1 at the end; payments are higher." %}

{% trans "Amount financed" %}

{% trans "The depreciation (cost minus residual) that you pay over the term. Interest is applied to this amount." %}
