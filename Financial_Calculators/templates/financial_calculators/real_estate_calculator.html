{% trans "Key Metrics" %}

  • {% trans "Cap Rate" %}: {% trans "NOI ÷ Purchase Price. Higher means better yield (ignoring financing)." %}

  • {% trans "DSCR" %}: {% trans "Debt service coverage ratio; NOI ÷ annual debt service. Lenders often require ≥ 1.25." %}
