{% trans "What is the Refinance Calculator?" %}

{% blocktrans %}This calculator helps you decide whether refinancing your mortgage makes sense. You enter your current loan balance, rate, payment, and months remaining, then the new loan rate and term. It computes your new monthly payment, break-even (how many months until closing costs are recovered by savings), and lifetime savings compared to staying with your current loan.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}You can add closing costs and points, choose to roll costs into the new loan, and optionally include cash-out. The recommendation considers both break-even time and total cost over the life of the loans.{% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Key Formulas" %}

{% trans "New monthly payment uses the standard amortization formula. Break-even and savings:" %}

{% trans "New payment" %}: PMT = P × [r(1+r)^n] ÷ [(1+r)^n − 1]

P = new loan amount, r = monthly rate, n = new term in months

Break-even (months) = Closing costs ÷ Monthly savings

{% trans "Lifetime savings = Current remaining total − (New total payments + closing costs if not rolled)." %}

{% trans "Key Concepts" %}

{% trans "Break-even" %}

{% trans "The number of months until your monthly savings have paid back the closing costs. If you sell or refinance again before break-even, you may not recover the costs." %}

{% trans "Closing costs" %}

{% trans "Fees and points paid at closing. Rolling them into the loan avoids upfront cash but increases the new loan amount and total interest." %}

{% trans "Lifetime savings" %}

{% trans "Total remaining cost of the current loan minus the total cost of the new loan (including closing costs if paid upfront). Positive means refinancing saves money overall." %}

{% trans "How to Use This Calculator" %}

  1. {% trans "Enter your current mortgage balance, rate, monthly payment, and months remaining (from your statement or lender)." %}
  2. {% trans "Enter the new rate and term you are considering." %}
  3. {% trans "Add estimated closing costs and points; check 'Roll closing costs into loan' if you will finance them." %}
  4. {% trans "Optionally add cash-out amount. Click Calculate to see recommendation, savings, and charts." %}
  5. {% trans "Use the Balance Over Time chart to compare how quickly each loan pays down." %}

{% trans "Understanding Your Results" %}

{% trans "Monthly savings" %}

{% trans "Break-even" %}

{% trans "Months until savings equal closing costs. Shorter break-even is better if you might move or refinance again." %}

{% trans "Lifetime savings" %}

{% trans "Total cost difference over the life of the loans. Positive means the new loan costs less in total." %}

{% trans "Recommendation" %}

{% trans "Based on break-even and lifetime savings. Consider your plans (how long you will stay) before deciding." %}
