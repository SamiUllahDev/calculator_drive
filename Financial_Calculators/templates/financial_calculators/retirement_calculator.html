{% trans "What is Retirement Readiness?" %}

{% blocktrans %}Retirement readiness measures whether your projected savings and income can support your desired lifestyle in retirement. This calculator projects your balance at retirement using your contributions and assumed returns, then estimates how long your funds will last with inflation-adjusted withdrawals. The 4%% rule suggests you can withdraw about 4%% of your nest egg annually.{% endblocktrans %}
