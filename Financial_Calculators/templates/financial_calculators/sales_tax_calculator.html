{% trans "What is Sales Tax?" %}

{% blocktrans %}Sales tax is a consumption tax charged at the point of purchase. In the US, rates vary by state and often by locality. Some states have no sales tax. This calculator helps you add tax to a pre-tax price, work backwards from a total to find the pre-tax amount and tax, or determine the effective tax rate when you know both amounts.{% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Total = Price × (1 + Rate)   |   Tax = Total − Price   |   Rate = (Total − Price) ÷ Price" %}

{% trans "Add Tax uses the first formula; Extract Tax solves for Price and Tax from Total and Rate; Find Rate solves for the effective rate from Price and Total." %}

{% trans "Key Concepts" %}

{% trans "Add Tax" %}

{% trans "You have a price before tax and a tax rate. The calculator adds the tax to get the total you pay." %}

{% trans "Extract Tax" %}

{% trans "You have a total (including tax) and the tax rate. The calculator splits it into pre-tax amount and tax amount." %}

{% trans "Find Rate" %}

{% trans "You have the price before tax and the total after tax. The calculator finds the effective tax rate (and tax amount)." %}
