{% trans "Complete User Guide" %}

{% trans "What is the Student Loan Calculator?" %}

{% trans "This calculator estimates your monthly student loan payment, total interest, and payoff date for federal or private student loans. Enter your total balance, interest rate, and repayment term. You can add an optional extra monthly payment to see how much interest you save and how many years earlier you pay off the loan." %}

{% trans "Results include a balance-over-time chart, a principal vs. interest breakdown chart, a comparison of repayment plans (Standard, Extended, Graduated, Aggressive), and a full payment schedule (monthly or yearly view)." %}

{% trans "Key Metrics" %}

{% trans "Monthly Payment" %}

{% trans "The fixed amount due each month. Shorter terms mean higher payments but less total interest." %}

{% trans "Total Interest" %}

{% trans "The total cost of borrowing over the life of the loan. Extra payments reduce this amount." %}

{% trans "Payoff Date" %}

{% trans "When the loan is fully paid. With extra payments, this date moves earlier." %}

{% trans "How to Use This Calculator" %}

  1. {% trans "Enter your total student loan balance (from your servicer or statement)." %}
  2. {% trans "Enter the interest rate and choose repayment term (e.g. 10 years Standard, 25 years Extended)." %}
  3. {% trans "Optionally enter an extra monthly payment to see savings and earlier payoff." %}
  4. {% trans "Click Calculate to see monthly payment, total interest, payoff date, charts, plan comparison, and schedule." %}
  5. {% trans "Switch between Monthly and Yearly views in the payment schedule." %}

{% trans "Understanding Your Results" %}

{% trans "The Compare Repayment Plans table shows Standard (10-year), Extended (25-year), Graduated, and Aggressive (5-year) options so you can see how term length affects payment and total cost. Extra payment savings show how much interest you save and how many years earlier you pay off when you add optional extra principal." %}

{% trans "Understanding the Charts" %}

{% trans "Balance over time:" %} {% trans "Shows how your balance decreases. If you entered an extra payment, two lines compare standard vs. with-extra-payment payoff." %}

{% trans "Payment breakdown:" %} {% trans "Doughnut chart of principal vs. interest so you see the share of total payments that go to the loan vs. borrowing cost." %}

{% trans "Formulas" %}

{% trans "Monthly Payment (M)" %}

M = P × [r(1+r)^n] / [(1+r)^n − 1]

{% trans "P = loan amount, r = monthly rate (APR ÷ 12), n = number of months" %}
