{% trans "What is VAT?" %}

{% blocktrans %}VAT (Value Added Tax) is a consumption tax added at each stage of production or distribution. It is used in over 160 countries. GST (Goods and Services Tax) is similar and used in Australia, Canada, India, and others. This calculator lets you add VAT to a net price, remove VAT from a gross price, or find the VAT amount from either.{% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Gross = Net × (1 + VAT%)   |   Net = Gross ÷ (1 + VAT%)   |   VAT = Gross − Net" %}
