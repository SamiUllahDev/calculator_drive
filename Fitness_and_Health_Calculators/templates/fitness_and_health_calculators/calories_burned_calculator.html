{% trans "What are MET Values?" %}

{% trans "MET (Metabolic Equivalent of Task) is a unit used to estimate the energy cost of physical activities. One MET is defined as the energy cost of sitting quietly, which is approximately 1 calorie per kilogram of body weight per hour." %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %}MET values allow you to compare the intensity of different activities. For example, an activity with a MET value of 4 means you're burning 4 times the energy you would burn while sitting quietly. Your resting metabolic rate is the foundation — calculate yours with our{% endblocktrans %} {% trans "BMR Calculator" %}.

{% trans "How Calories are Calculated" %}

{% trans "The formula used to calculate calories burned is:" %}

{% trans "Calories Burned = MET × Weight (kg) × Time (hours)" %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %}This formula takes into account your body weight, the intensity of the activity (MET value), and the duration of the exercise. For your total daily energy expenditure including all activities, use our{% endblocktrans %} {% trans "TDEE Calculator" %} {% blocktrans trimmed %}or our{% endblocktrans %} {% trans "Calorie Calculator" %}.
