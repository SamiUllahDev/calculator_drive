{% trans "What are Macronutrients?" %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %}Macronutrients (macros) are the three main nutrients your body needs in large amounts: protein, carbohydrates, and fats. Each provides energy (calories) and serves unique functions in your body. Don't know your calorie target? Use our{% endblocktrans %} {% trans "Calorie Calculator" %} {% trans "or" %} {% trans "TDEE Calculator" %} {% trans "to find it first." %}

{% trans "Total Calories = Protein + Fat + Carbs" %}

{% trans "Protein and carbohydrates provide 4 calories per gram, while fat provides 9 calories per gram. Understanding your macro breakdown helps you optimize your diet for your specific goals." %}

{% trans "Macronutrient Functions" %}

{% trans "Protein (4 cal/g)" %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %}Essential for muscle building, repair, and maintenance. Supports immune function, hormone production, and satiety. Recommended: 0.8-2.2g per kg body weight depending on activity level. Get detailed targets with our{% endblocktrans %} {% trans "Protein Calculator" %}.

{% trans "Fat (9 cal/g)" %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %}Provides energy, supports hormone production, aids vitamin absorption, and protects organs. Essential for brain health. Recommended: 20-35% of total calories. Plan your fat intake with our{% endblocktrans %} {% trans "Fat Intake Calculator" %}.

{% trans "Carbohydrates (4 cal/g)" %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %}Primary energy source for your body and brain. Supports exercise performance and recovery. Recommended: 45-65% of total calories for most people, lower for low-carb diets. Calculate your carb needs with our{% endblocktrans %} {% trans "Carbohydrate Calculator" %}.
