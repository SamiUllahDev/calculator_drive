{% trans "What Does It Mean to Be Overweight?" %}

{% trans "Being overweight means having a Body Mass Index (BMI) of 25 or higher. BMI is calculated using your weight and height to estimate body fat. While BMI is a useful screening tool, it doesn't directly measure body fat or account for muscle mass." %}

BMI = Weight (kg) ÷ Height (m)²

{% trans "BMI categories: Underweight (<18.5), Normal Weight (18.5-24.9), Overweight (25-29.9), Obese (≥30). Being overweight or obese increases the risk of various health conditions." %}

{% trans "BMI Categories" %}

{% trans "Underweight: BMI < 18.5" %}

{% trans "Includes severe thinness (<16), moderate thinness (16-17), and mild thinness (17-18.5). May need to gain weight. Consult with a healthcare provider." %}

{% trans "Normal Weight: BMI 18.5-24.9" %}

{% trans "Healthy weight range. Continue maintaining a balanced diet and regular physical activity." %}

{% trans "Overweight: BMI 25-29.9" %}

{% trans "Above healthy weight range. Consider a balanced diet and regular exercise to reach a healthy weight." %}

{% trans "Obese: BMI ≥ 30" %}

{% trans "Includes Obese Class I (30-35), Class II (35-40), and Class III (>40). Consult with healthcare professionals for a comprehensive weight management plan." %}
