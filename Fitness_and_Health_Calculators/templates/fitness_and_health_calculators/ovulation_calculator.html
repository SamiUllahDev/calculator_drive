{% trans "What is Ovulation?" %}

{% trans "Ovulation is the process in which a mature egg is released from one of the ovaries. This typically occurs once per menstrual cycle, approximately 14 days before the start of your next period. The egg travels down the fallopian tube where it can be fertilized by sperm." %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %}Understanding when you ovulate is crucial for both conception planning and natural family planning. The fertile window includes the days leading up to and including ovulation, when conception is most likely to occur. If you're planning for pregnancy, you may also find our{% endblocktrans %} {% trans "Conception Calculator" %} {% trans "and" %} {% trans "Period Calculator" %} {% trans "helpful." %}

{% trans "Menstrual Cycle Phases" %}

{% trans "Menstruation (Days 1-5)" %}

{% trans "The uterine lining is shed. This marks day 1 of your cycle. Typically lasts 3-7 days." %}

{% trans "Follicular Phase (Days 1-13)" %}

{% trans "Follicles in the ovaries begin to mature. Estrogen levels rise, preparing the uterine lining for potential pregnancy." %}

{% trans "Ovulation (Day 14, approximately)" %}

{% trans "A mature egg is released from the ovary. This is your most fertile day. The egg survives for 12-24 hours after release." %}

{% trans "Fertile Window (5 days before + ovulation + 1 day after)" %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %}The best time for conception. Sperm can survive up to 5 days in the female reproductive tract, so having intercourse in the days leading up to ovulation increases chances of conception. Once pregnant, estimate your baby's arrival with our{% endblocktrans %} {% trans "Due Date Calculator" %}.

{% trans "Luteal Phase (Days 15-28)" %}

{% trans "After ovulation, the corpus luteum produces progesterone. If pregnancy doesn't occur, hormone levels drop and menstruation begins." %}

{% trans "Fertile Window" %}

{% trans "The 6-7 day period when conception is most likely. This includes 5 days before ovulation, the day of ovulation, and 1 day after. Sperm can survive up to 5 days, so having intercourse during this window maximizes chances of conception." %}

{% trans "Signs of Ovulation" %}

{% trans "Common signs that ovulation may be occurring:" %}

  • {% trans "Change in cervical mucus (becomes clear, stretchy, like egg white)" %}
  • {% trans "Slight increase in basal body temperature" %}
  • {% trans "Mild pelvic or abdominal pain (mittelschmerz)" %}
  • {% trans "Increased libido" %}
  • {% trans "Positive ovulation predictor kit (OPK) result" %}
