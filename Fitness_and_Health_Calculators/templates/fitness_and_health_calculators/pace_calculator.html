{% trans "What is Running Pace?" %}

{% trans "Running pace is the time it takes to cover a specific distance, typically expressed as minutes per kilometer (min/km) or minutes per mile (min/mi). It's a fundamental metric for runners to track performance, plan training, and predict race times." %}

{% trans "Pace = Time ÷ Distance" %}

{% trans "For example, if you run 5 kilometers in 30 minutes, your pace is 6 minutes per kilometer (30 ÷ 5 = 6 min/km)." %}
