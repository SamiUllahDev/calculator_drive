{% trans "What is Conception?" %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %}Conception occurs when a sperm fertilizes an egg, typically happening during ovulation. This is the moment when pregnancy begins. The fertilized egg then travels to the uterus and implants in the uterine lining, usually 6-12 days after conception. Track your ovulation with our{% endblocktrans %} {% trans "Ovulation Calculator" %}.

{% blocktrans trimmed %}Understanding your conception date helps you track your pregnancy timeline more accurately and provides insight into when important milestones occur. For cycle tracking, also use our{% endblocktrans %} {% trans "Period Calculator" %}.

{% trans "Key Concepts" %}

{% trans "Conception Date" %}

{% trans "The date when the sperm fertilized the egg. This typically occurs during ovulation, approximately 14 days after the start of your last menstrual period (for a 28-day cycle)." %}

{% trans "Fertile Window" %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %}The 6-7 day period when conception is most likely. Includes 5 days before ovulation, the day of ovulation, and 1 day after. Plan with our{% endblocktrans %} {% trans "Conception Calculator" %}.

{% trans "Ovulation" %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %}The release of a mature egg from the ovary. This is your most fertile day. The egg survives for 12-24 hours after release. Track it precisely with our{% endblocktrans %} {% trans "Ovulation Calculator" %}.

{% trans "Implantation" %}

{% trans "When the fertilized egg attaches to the uterine lining. This typically occurs 6-12 days after conception. Implantation bleeding (light spotting) may occur but is not always present." %}

{% trans "Understanding Your Results" %}

{% trans "Conception Date" %}

{% trans "The estimated date when conception occurred. This is calculated based on your due date, LMP, or known conception date. Conception typically occurs during ovulation, approximately 14 days after the start of your last period." %}

{% trans "Fertile Window" %}

{% trans "The 6-7 day period when conception was most likely. This includes 5 days before ovulation, the day of ovulation, and 1 day after. Having intercourse during this window maximizes chances of conception." %}

{% trans "Implantation Window" %}

{% trans "The period when the fertilized egg typically implants in the uterine lining (6-12 days after conception). Some women may experience light spotting during this time, known as implantation bleeding." %}

{% trans "Weeks Pregnant vs Weeks Since Conception" %}

{% trans "Pregnancy is counted from the first day of your last menstrual period (LMP), not from conception. This is why you're considered 2 weeks pregnant at conception. Weeks since conception is the actual time since fertilization occurred." %}
