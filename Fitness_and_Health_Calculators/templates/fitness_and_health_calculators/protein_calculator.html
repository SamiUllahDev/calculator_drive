{% trans "What is Protein and Why Do You Need It?" %}

{% trans "Protein is one of the three macronutrients (along with carbohydrates and fats) essential for human health. It's made up of amino acids, which are the building blocks of muscles, tissues, enzymes, hormones, and many other important molecules in your body." %}

{% trans "Adequate protein intake is crucial for muscle maintenance and growth, immune function, hormone production, and overall health. Your protein needs vary based on your body weight, activity level, age, and fitness goals." %}

{% trans "Protein Recommendations by Activity Level" %}

{% trans "Sedentary (0.8 g/kg)" %}

{% trans "RDA minimum for adults. Suitable for people with little to no exercise. Office work, minimal physical activity." %}

{% trans "Light Activity (1.0 g/kg)" %}

{% trans "Light exercise 1-3 days per week. Walking, light jogging, or recreational activities." %}

{% trans "Moderate Activity (1.2 g/kg)" %}

{% trans "Moderate exercise 3-5 days per week. Regular workouts, sports, or active lifestyle." %}

{% trans "Active (1.4 g/kg)" %}

{% trans "Active exercise 6-7 days per week. Intense workouts, competitive sports, or physically demanding job." %}

{% trans "Very Active (1.6 g/kg)" %}

{% trans "Very active lifestyle. Physical job, daily intense exercise, or multiple training sessions." %}

{% trans "Elite Athlete (1.8 g/kg)" %}

{% trans "Elite athlete level. Professional training, multiple daily sessions, competition preparation." %}

{% trans "Protein Needs by Fitness Goal" %}

{% trans "Weight Loss (+20%)" %}

{% trans "Higher protein intake helps preserve muscle mass during calorie deficit and increases satiety, making it easier to stick to your diet." %}

{% trans "Maintain Weight (Standard)" %}

{% trans "Standard protein intake to maintain current muscle mass and support daily activities and recovery." %}

{% trans "Muscle Gain (+10%)" %}

{% trans "Slightly higher protein intake to support muscle growth and recovery from training. Essential for building new muscle tissue." %}

{% trans "Cutting (+30%)" %}

{% trans "High protein intake to preserve muscle mass during aggressive fat loss phase. Critical for bodybuilders and athletes cutting weight." %}
