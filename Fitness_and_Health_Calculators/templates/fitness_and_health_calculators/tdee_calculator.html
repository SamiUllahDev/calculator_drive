{% trans "What is TDEE?" %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %}Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE) is the total number of calories your body burns in a day. It includes your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) plus the calories burned through physical activity, digestion, and other daily activities. Knowing your TDEE is essential for planning your daily{% endblocktrans %} {% trans "calorie intake" %}.

{% blocktrans trimmed %}TDEE is calculated by multiplying your{% endblocktrans %} {% trans "BMR" %} {% trans "(the calories your body needs at rest) by an activity multiplier that accounts for your daily physical activity level." %}

{% trans "TDEE = BMR × Activity Multiplier" %}

{% trans "What is BMR?" %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %}Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) is the number of calories your body burns at rest to maintain basic physiological functions. Calculate yours precisely with our{% endblocktrans %} {% trans "BMR Calculator" %}.

{% blocktrans trimmed %}BMR accounts for 60-75% of your total daily calorie burn. It's influenced by your age, gender, height, weight, and{% endblocktrans %} {% trans "body fat percentage" %}.
