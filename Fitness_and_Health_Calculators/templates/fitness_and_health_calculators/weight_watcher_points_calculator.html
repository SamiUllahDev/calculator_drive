{% trans "What are Weight Watchers SmartPoints?" %}

{% trans "Weight Watchers SmartPoints is a point system that assigns values to foods based on their nutritional content. Unlike simple calorie counting, SmartPoints considers calories, saturated fat, sugar, and protein to encourage healthier food choices." %}

{% trans "Foods higher in calories, saturated fat, and sugar have more points, while foods higher in protein have fewer points. This system helps guide you toward nutrient-dense foods that support weight loss and healthy eating." %}

{% trans "SmartPoints Formula" %}

{% trans "The SmartPoints formula calculates points based on four nutritional factors:" %}

{% trans "Points = (Calories ÷ 33) + (Saturated Fat ÷ 4) + (Sugar ÷ 9) - (Protein ÷ 10)" %}

{% trans "Calories" %}: {% trans "Divided by 33. Higher calories = more points." %}

{% trans "Saturated Fat" %}: {% trans "Divided by 4. Higher saturated fat = more points." %}

{% trans "Sugar" %}: {% trans "Divided by 9. Higher sugar = more points." %}

{% trans "Protein" %}: {% trans "Divided by 10 and subtracted. Higher protein = fewer points (reduces total points)." %}

{% trans "Daily Points Allowance" %}

{% trans "Your daily points allowance is personalized based on your age, gender, height, weight, and activity level. Typical daily allowances range from 23 to 71 points." %}

{% trans "In addition to daily points, Weight Watchers members also receive weekly points (typically 14-28 points) that can be used throughout the week for special occasions or treats." %}
