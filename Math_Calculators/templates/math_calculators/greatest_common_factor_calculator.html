{% trans "What is GCD?" %}

{% trans "GCD (Greatest Common Divisor) or GCF (Greatest Common Factor) is the largest positive integer that divides each of the given numbers without leaving a remainder." %}

{% trans "For example, GCD of 24 and 36 is 12, because 12 is the largest number that divides both 24 and 36 evenly." %}

{% trans "What is LCM?" %}

{% trans "LCM (Least Common Multiple) is the smallest positive integer that is a multiple of each of the given numbers." %}

{% trans "For example, LCM of 24 and 36 is 72, because 72 is the smallest number that is divisible by both 24 and 36." %}
