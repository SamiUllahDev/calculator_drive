  • {% trans "Law of Sines" %}: a/sin(A) = b/sin(B) = c/sin(C)
  • {% trans "Law of Cosines" %}: a² = b² + c² - 2bc cos(A)
  • {% trans "Heron's Formula" %}: Area = √[s(s-a)(s-b)(s-c)], where s = (a+b+c)/2
  • {% trans "Area (SAS)" %}: Area = 0.5 × a × c × sin(B)
