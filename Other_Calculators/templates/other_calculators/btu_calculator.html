{% trans "What is BTU?" %}

{% blocktrans trimmed %}BTU stands for British Thermal Unit. It is the amount of energy needed to raise the temperature of one pound of water by one degree Fahrenheit. HVAC systems — heaters, air conditioners, and furnaces — are rated in BTU per hour. Knowing how many BTU your room needs helps you pick the right size system, save energy, and stay comfortable. To determine your room's area, use our{% endblocktrans %} {% trans "Square Footage Calculator" %}.

{% trans "Room Size" %}: {% trans "BTU = L × W × BTU/sq ft × Insulation × Climate" %}

{% trans "Area BTU" %}: {% trans "BTU = Area × BTU/sq ft × Insulation × Climate" %}

{% trans "Cooling Load" %}: {% trans "BTU = Base + (Windows × 1000) + (People × 400) + (Watts × 3.412)" %}
