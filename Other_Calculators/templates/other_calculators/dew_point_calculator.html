{% trans "What is Dew Point?" %}

{% trans "Dew point is the temperature at which air becomes saturated with water vapor and condensation begins to form. It is a measure of atmospheric moisture. When the air temperature drops to the dew point, relative humidity reaches 100%, and water vapor condenses into liquid water (dew). The dew point is always less than or equal to the air temperature." %}

{% trans "Magnus Formula" %}

{% trans "Dew Point Formula:" %}

Td = (B × α) / (A - α)

{% trans "Where:" %}

  • α = (A × T) / (B + T) + ln(RH/100)
  • A = 17.27
  • B = 237.7°C
  • T = {% trans "Temperature" %} (°C)
  • RH = {% trans "Relative Humidity" %} (%)
  • Td = {% trans "Dew Point" %} (°C)
