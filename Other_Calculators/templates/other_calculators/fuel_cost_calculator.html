{% trans "Fuel Efficiency Formulas" %}

{% trans "MPG (Miles Per Gallon):" %}

MPG = Distance (miles) / Fuel (gallons)

{% trans "L/100km (Liters Per 100 Kilometers):" %}

L/100km = (Fuel (liters) / Distance (km)) × 100

{% trans "Conversion:" %}

  • MPG = 235.214 / (L/100km)
  • L/100km = 235.214 / MPG

{% trans "MPG (Miles Per Gallon) is the US standard for measuring fuel efficiency, representing how many miles a vehicle can travel on one gallon of fuel. L/100km (Liters Per 100 Kilometers) is the metric standard, representing how many liters of fuel are needed to travel 100 kilometers. Lower L/100km values indicate better fuel efficiency." %}
