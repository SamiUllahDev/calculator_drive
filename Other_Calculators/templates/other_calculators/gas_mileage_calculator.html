{% trans "What is Gas Mileage?" %}

{% blocktrans %}Gas mileage, measured in MPG (miles per gallon), indicates how far a vehicle can travel on one gallon of fuel. Higher MPG means better fuel economy, lower fuel costs, and less environmental impact. Tracking your gas mileage helps you budget for trips, compare vehicles, and monitor your driving efficiency over time.{% endblocktrans %}
