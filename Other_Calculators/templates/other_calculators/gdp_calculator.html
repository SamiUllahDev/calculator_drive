{% trans "What is GDP?" %}

{% blocktrans %}Gross Domestic Product (GDP) is the total monetary value of all finished goods and services produced within a country's borders in a specific time period. It serves as a comprehensive measure of economic performance and is used to compare economic output of different countries. GDP can be calculated using expenditure, income, and production (value-added) approaches.{% endblocktrans %}
