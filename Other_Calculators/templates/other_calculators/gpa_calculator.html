{% trans "What is GPA? Understanding Grade Point Average" %}

{% blocktrans %}Grade Point Average (GPA) is a standardized numerical representation of your academic performance. It is calculated by dividing the total quality points you have earned by the total number of credit hours you have attempted. Colleges, universities, and high schools use GPA to evaluate student achievement, determine eligibility for scholarships and honors, and make admissions decisions for graduate programs.{% endblocktrans %}
