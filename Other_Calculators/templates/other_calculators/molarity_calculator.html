{% trans "What is Molarity?" %}

{% blocktrans %}Molarity (M) is a measure of solution concentration defined as the number of moles of solute per liter of solution. It is the most common unit for expressing concentration in chemistry. A 1 M solution contains exactly 1 mole of solute dissolved in enough solvent to make 1 liter of total solution.{% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "All Formulas at a Glance" %}

{% trans "Molarity" %}: M = n / V

{% trans "Moles" %}: n = M × V

{% trans "Volume" %}: V = n / M

{% trans "Moles from Mass" %}: n = mass / MW

{% trans "Dilution" %}: M1 × V1 = M2 × V2
