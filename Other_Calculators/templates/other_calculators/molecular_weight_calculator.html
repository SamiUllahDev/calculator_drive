{% trans "What is Molecular Weight?" %}

{% blocktrans %}Molecular weight (also called molar mass) is the total mass of one mole of a compound, calculated by summing the atomic weights of every atom in its chemical formula. It is expressed in grams per mole (g/mol) and is fundamental to converting between mass and moles in stoichiometry, solution preparation, and analytical chemistry.{% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Formula" %}

{% trans "Molecular Weight" %}: MW = Σ (Atomic Weight × Count)

{% trans "Mass %" %}: % = (Element Mass / MW) × 100
