{% trans "What is Molecular Weight?" %}

{% trans "Molecular weight (also called molar mass) is the sum of the atomic weights of all atoms in a molecule. It is expressed in grams per mole (g/mol) and is used to convert between mass and moles in chemical calculations." %}
