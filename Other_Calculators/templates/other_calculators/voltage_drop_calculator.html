{% trans "What is Voltage Drop?" %}

{% blocktrans %}Voltage drop is the reduction in voltage in an electrical circuit due to the resistance of the conductors. It's important to keep voltage drop within acceptable limits to ensure proper equipment operation. The NEC recommends no more than 3% for branch circuits and 5% for combined feeder and branch circuits.{% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Key Formulas" %}

{% trans "DC" %}: VD = I × R × 2

{% trans "Single-Phase AC" %}: VD = I × R × 2 × PF

{% trans "Three-Phase AC" %}: VD = I × R × √3 × PF

{% trans "Resistance" %}: R = (ρ × L) / A
